About Shakalya Clinic

Rooted in Satara, built on plain-spoken medicine

Shakalya Clinic opened its doors in Shaniwar Peth with a simple goal — give this neighbourhood a clinic it could rely on for everyday illness, ongoing conditions and honest guidance. Fifteen years on, that's still the whole plan.

Our mission

To make thorough, unhurried medical care accessible to every family in Satara — in person, online, or at their doorstep.

Our approach

Every visit starts with listening. We explain conditions in plain language, involve you in the decision, and follow up — we don't just prescribe and move on.
